Favoutite Football Clubs of Shubhodip

My Favourite Football Clubs from around the World

SC East Bengal

This is my boyhood club from the city of Calcutta, India
It is a part of the Bangal Identity of Bengalis - the ones who emigrated from present day Bangladesh to India
Mohun Bagan are their fiercest rivals representing the Ghoti Community

FC Barcelona

This is the club I grew up watching, belonging to the city of Barcelona, Spain
It is a symbol of the Catalan Democratic Resistance against the Spanish Imperialism of Francisco Franco
Real Madrid are their fiercest rivals representing the Castille Region

AC Milan

This is a historic club from the city of Milan, Italy
It is a part of the Working Class Milanista Identity
Inter Milan are their fiercest rivals forming one half of the Derby Della Madonnina
